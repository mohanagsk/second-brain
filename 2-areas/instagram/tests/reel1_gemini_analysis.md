## 5. REPLICATION TEMPLATE

### Universal Structure

```
[SECTION 1: HOOK] (0-3s)
"[Informal greeting], [solution type] ka naam dm me to comment '[trigger word]'"

[SECTION 2: PROBLEM SETUP] (3-10s)
"So guys, today I'm going to show you something [intensity adjective]. 
You know [widely experienced frustration]? Like [specific examples]?"

[SECTION 3: SOLUTION + BENEFIT] (10-20s)
"I found [solution name/type] where you can actually [key action/benefit]. 
And the best part? [Quantifiable advantage vs. known alternative]."

[SECTION 4: PROOF + OBJECTION HANDLING] (20-30s)
"I [verification action] myself. [Positive outcome 1]. [Positive outcome 2]. 
[Biggest objection addressed]."

[SECTION 5: REVEAL + ACCESS PATH] (30-40s)
"[Solution name spelled phonetically]. Link is in bio, 
but if you want me to DM you directly..."

[SECTION 6: FINAL CTA] (40-50s)
"Just comment '[trigger word]' below and I'll send you [the thing]. 
Trust me, [transformation promise]. 
Comment '[trigger word]' right now!"
```

### Variable Options

**[Informal greeting]**: Bhai | Dude | Guys | Friend | Yo
**[Trigger word]**: Free | Link | Send | Code | Access | Info
**[Intensity adjective]**: crazy | insane | game-changing | unbelievable
**[Known alternative]**: Amazon | Popular Brand | Traditional Method

### Example Application

**Topic**: AI Writing Tools

"Guys, AI tool ka naam dm me to comment 'Write'"

"You know how expensive ChatGPT Plus is? Like ₹2000/month? I found this platform where you get 10 AI tools in one place. And the best part? ₹299/month."

## ACTIONABLE RECOMMENDATIONS

For creators attempting to replicate:

1. ✅ **Test comment-driven CTAs** across multiple content types
2. ✅ **Front-load asks** (don't wait 30s to request action)
3. ✅ **Use single-word triggers** (reduce cognitive friction)
4. ✅ **Spell out names phonetically** (aids recall/search)
5. ✅ **Layer 3 emotional triggers** minimum (FOMO + trust + curiosity)
6. ⚠️ **Ensure DM capacity** (manual or automated) to fulfill promise
7. ⚠️ **A/B test trigger words** (Free vs Link vs Send)

**Risk Factor**: Over-reliance on DM promise may create fulfillment burden at scale.
