## HOOK FORMULAS (Mix & Match)

### Formula 1: Command + Implied Value
```
[Casual address], [thing they want] ka naam dm me to comment '[trigger word]'
```
**Examples**:
- "Dost, iss app ka naam chahiye to comment 'Link'"
- "Bro, this hack ka video dm me to type 'Hack'"

### Formula 2: Skip/Bypass Pattern
```
Skip [pain point] with [solution approach]
```
**Examples**:
- "Skip Zomato surge prices with THIS app"
- "Bypass OTP wait with [feature name]"

### Formula 3: Only This [Product]
```
Only this [product] [unique benefit nobody else offers]
```
**Examples**:
- "Only this keyboard tells you when someone's watching"
- "Only this extension blocks YouTube ads (actually works)"

### Formula 4: Meet [Name] Story
```
Meet [Name]. He/She never [expected action]. Yet [surprising outcome]...
```
**Examples**:
- "Meet Rahul. He never paid for Spotify. Yet he has zero ads..."
- "Meet Priya. She never used LinkedIn. Yet she got 3 job offers..."

### Formula 5: [X] is a SCAM?
```
'[Seemingly positive thing]' is a SCAM? [emoji]
```
**Examples**:
- "'Free' VPNs are selling your data"
- "Premium earphones are a scam? ₹500 vs ₹5000 test"

- ✅ "Yaar, this app is insane..."

## HOOK TESTING MATRIX

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| 3-sec retention | >50% | First frame hold rate |
| Save rate | >3% | Indicates rewatch value |
| Comment rate | >1% | CTA effectiveness |
| Share rate | >0.5% | Viral potential |

**A/B Test Variables**:
- Word choice (Bhai vs Dost vs Yaar)
- CTA word (Free vs Link vs Naam)
- Question vs Statement
- Hindi vs English ratio

## ANTI-PATTERNS (Avoid These)

| Anti-Pattern | Why It Fails | Example |
|--------------|--------------|---------|
| **Generic greeting** | No urgency | "Hey everyone, in this video..." |
| **Self-intro first** | Nobody cares yet | "My name is [X], I'm a [Y]..." |
| **Multi-step CTA** | Too much friction | "Go to my bio, click the link, then..." |
| **Asking for engagement** | Feels desperate | "Please like and follow guys..." |
| **Overpromising** | Kills trust | "This will change your life forever..." |

---

## ADAPTATION NOTES

**For Tech Content**:
- Lead with outcome, not process
- Use product names early (SEO)
- "Only this" works better than "Best"

**For Life Hacks**:
- "Skip/Bypass" outperforms "How to"
- Show, don't explain in hook
- Quantity signals (tested 50X) build trust

**For Storytelling**:
- Name + contradiction = instant curiosity
- "Meet [X], they never [Y]" is repeatable
- Hold reveal until 15-20s mark
